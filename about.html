About ICINC 2020

ICINC-2020 is an international conference broadly addressing Internet of Things, Next generation networks and cloud computing. The conference will address the trends, challenges and future roadmaps within a conglomerate of existing and novel wireless technologies and recent advances in information theory and its applications. This conference will feature a comprehensive technical program including special sessions and short courses.

The goal of ICINC-2020 to provide the outstanding forum for researchers, practitioner, user exchange ideas, techniques and tools. This conference emphasize on promoting high level of interaction between theoretical and practical and applied technologies of Internet of Thing, Next generation networks and cloud computing.

This conference will serve as platform to cover advanced aspect and approaches of Internet of Thing, Next generation networks and cloud computing.

The conference will strengthen the relationship and tie with other institution and international scientist and facilitate joint research programmers in this highly challenging and demanding area.

ICINC is inviting, soliciting original previously unpublished and high quality research papers addressing research challenges and advances in the topic.
